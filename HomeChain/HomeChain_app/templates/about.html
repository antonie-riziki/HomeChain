About HomeChain

Decentralizing Domestic Work

Our Mission

HomeChain is a decentralized platform connecting domestic workers (nannies, housekeepers, caregivers, etc.) with employers through a secure, transparent, and intelligent system. We combine off-chain job discovery with on-chain smart contract agreements to ensure fair treatment and secure payments for all parties.

The Problem We Solve

Daily domestic workers rely heavily on word-of-mouth referrals, informal networks, and physical "tarmacking" to find opportunities. This results in:

  • check_circle Job insecurity and inconsistent income
  • check_circle Wage exploitation and lack of payment protection
  • check_circle No verified work history or career growth opportunities
  • check_circle Safety risks for both workers and employers

Built on Stellar Blockchain

HomeChain leverages the Stellar blockchain for fast, low-cost transactions and smart contract functionality through Soroban. Our platform integrates blockchain technology seamlessly, providing the benefits of decentralization without compromising user experience.
