How do I create an account?

Click "Get Started" on the homepage, choose whether you're an employer or worker, and fill out the registration form. Your Stellar wallet will be created automatically.

What is a Stellar wallet?

A Stellar wallet is a blockchain account that allows you to send and receive payments securely. HomeChain creates one for you automatically when you register.

business For Employers

How do I post a job?

Navigate to your dashboard and click "Post a Job". Fill in the job details including title, description, skills required, and payment terms.

How does escrow payment work?

When you hire a worker, funds are held in a secure escrow smart contract. The payment is released to the worker only after you confirm the work is completed satisfactorily.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work?

You can dispute the contract before releasing payment. Our dispute resolution system will mediate to find a fair solution.

work For Workers

How do I find jobs?

Browse available jobs in the "Browse Jobs" section. You can filter by location, skills, and payment range to find opportunities that match your profile.

When do I get paid?

Payment is held in escrow and automatically released to your wallet when the employer confirms your work is complete. No more chasing payments!

How do I build my reputation?

Complete jobs successfully and receive ratings from employers. Your verified work history and ratings build your profile credibility.

payments Payments & Blockchain

What currency is used?

HomeChain uses Kenyan Shillings (KShs) for all transactions. Payments are processed through the Stellar blockchain for secure and transparent transfers.

Are there transaction fees?

Stellar blockchain fees are minimal (fractions of a cent). HomeChain charges a small platform fee on completed contracts.

Is my payment secure?

Yes! Smart contracts on the blockchain ensure payments are secure and transparent. Funds cannot be moved without contract conditions being met.
